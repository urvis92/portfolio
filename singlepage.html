WHY THEY CELEBRATE HALLOWEEN

Graphic/Publishing/Editorial Design

Why they Celebrate Halloween is inspired by a curosity I developed my first time being in the United States during the holiday. The journey took me forward to learn the deep rooted history of the holiday and why it is celebrated. It was quite evident that the holiday though had lost some of its meaning and become a commercial holiday like a lot of other festivals. The booklet is a take on the old and new, the history and present commercialism.
