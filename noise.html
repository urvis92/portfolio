NOISE

Graphic Design/Silkcreen Printing

Noise, is a experimental interpretation of the imaginary and unseen soundwaves. Using 3 differnt methods and materials the project is my interpretation of how sounds waves travel around us. While the first 1st is an experiment of the effect of overlays projected with the same constant color and material, the next 2 try to portray layers of color and position through the digital and handmade methods using the physical form of soundawaves.
