While women around the worl fight for rights for equality women in india are still struggling to fight for basic rights of humanity. A blanket of customs, cultural and religious laws deny them a right to individuality, passion, education and freedom. Under this beautiful blanket lies the story of millions of women who suffer gender bias in ways that are unimaginable. It is now hoever the time to rip apart these so called ideals and pillars of cultural and start listenig to our women.
